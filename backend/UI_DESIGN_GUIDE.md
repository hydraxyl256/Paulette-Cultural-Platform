## Color Application Guide

### Text Colors
- **Headlines**: `text-slate-900` (darkest)
- **Body text**: `text-slate-700` (dark)
- **Secondary text**: `text-slate-600` (medium)
- **Placeholders**: `text-slate-500` (light)
- **Disabled**: `text-slate-400` (very light)

### Background Colors
- **Primary action**: `bg-indigo-600` вҶ’ hover `bg-indigo-700`
- **Secondary action**: `bg-slate-100` вҶ’ hover `bg-slate-200`
- **Danger action**: `bg-red-600` вҶ’ hover `bg-red-700`
- **Success badge**: `bg-green-100` text `text-green-800`
- **Card background**: `bg-white`
- **Page background**: `bg-slate-50`

### Border Colors
- **Light borders**: `border-slate-200`
- **Dividers**: `border-slate-200`
- **Active/Focus**: `border-indigo-500`
